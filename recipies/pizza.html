Pizza

Pizza is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. It is a great dish to make for a crowd or to make and freeze for later. It is a versatile dish that can be made with a variety of ingredients. Here is a basic recipe for pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C). If you are using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface until it is the desired size and thickness. Place dough on a pizza peel or baking sheet that has been dusted with cornmeal.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce. Top with pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers. Sprinkle parmesan cheese over top.
  4. Transfer pizza to preheated pizza stone or place baking sheet in oven. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Remove pizza from oven and let cool for a few minutes. Sprinkle chopped basil over top before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your Pizza!
